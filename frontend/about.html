About Us

The story behind FaceVerseAi

Real-time AI-powered avatar streaming for creators, streamers, virtual entertainers and online communities.

What we build

FaceVerseAi is a real-time AI-powered virtual avatar and live character streaming platform built for creators, streamers, entertainment, and digital experiences.

Our technology allows users to transform their live camera feed into stylized AI-generated characters during supported live streams, virtual meetings, and content sessions using broadcasting tools such as OBS Studio.

Who it's for

FaceVerseAi is designed to provide immersive and creative digital experiences for:

  • Content creators
  • Live streamers
  • Virtual entertainers
  • Online communities
  • Social media creators

Users access the platform through a prepaid credit system that enables real-time AI rendering sessions and related digital services.

Our mission

Our mission is to make AI-powered virtual presence technology accessible, fast, and enjoyable for creators around the world.

We are committed to responsible AI usage, platform safety, user privacy, and compliance with applicable laws and platform policies.
